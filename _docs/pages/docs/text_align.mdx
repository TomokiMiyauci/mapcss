## Basic usage

### Setting the text alignment

Control the text alignment of an element using the `text-left`, `text-center`, `text-right`, and `text-justify` utilities.

<Example p="none">
  <div className="px-4 sm:px-0">
    <div className="mx-auto max-w-xl bg-white shadow-xl p-8 text-slate-700 text-sm leading-6 sm:text-base sm:leading-7 dark:bg-slate-800 dark:text-slate-400">
      <p className="text-left">
        So I started to walk into the water. I won't lie to you boys, I was
        terrified. But I pressed on, and as I made my way past the breakers a
        strange calm came over me. I don't know if it was divine intervention or
        the kinship of all living things but I tell you Jerry at that moment, I{" "}
        <em>was</em> a marine biologist.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</Example>

```html
<p className="**text-left** ...">So I started to walk into the water...</p>
```
